Christian fiction best sellers

By willwhim

October 7, 2005

I was browsing Amazon’s list of best sellers in Christian fiction tonigh to see if books by a friend, Neta Jackson, were listed. They were! The Yada Yada Prayer Group is 96 today, and her upcoming The Yada Yada Prayer Group Gets Tough is at 24.

I was amused to see The Canterbury Tales listed at 38–that’s an impressive back list.
