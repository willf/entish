Claude Shannon

By willwhim

August 12, 2005

Random notes about Claude Shannon., the founder of information theory. Although Wikipedia says he was born in Petoskey, Michigan, a statue of him was erected in Gaylord Michigan as part of six statues erected in his honor, including the University of Michigan. The lispmeister found the TeX source for Shannon’s seminal A Mathematical Theory of Communication.
