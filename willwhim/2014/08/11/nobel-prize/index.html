The Nobel Prize of X

By willwhim

August 11, 2014

Awards that are “the Nobel Prize of” that field:

  • Math: The Fields Medal
  • Computer Science: The A.M. Turing Award
  • Food: The World Food Prize
  • Water: The Stockholm Water Prize
  • Education: The Harold W. McGraw, Jr. Prize in Education
  • Manufacturing: Shingo Prize for Excellence in Manufacturing
  • Geography: Le Lauréat Prix International de Géographie Vautrin Lud
  • Architecture: Pritzker Prize
  • Gastronomy: White Guide Global Gastronomy Award
  • Environmentalism: The Goldman Prize
  • Music: The Polar Music Prize
