A Fibonacci A Day — Special Pi Day Post

By willwhim

March 14, 2013

Binging for “Fibonacci” and “Pi,” I came across this page: Pi and the Fibonacci Numbers, which has the nice formula

pi/4 = Σk=1 arccot(F(2k+1)) summed to ∞, where F(n) is the _n_th Fibonacci number.

So, if you can compute the Fibonacci sequence, you can compute pi.
