The official syrup and hot sauce of the Montgomery Biscuits

By willwhim

March 15, 2007

Mark writes about serving pancakes for supper for the first time (and claims to be the first evah to blog about it).

And it reminded me how much I like Alaga syrup, “The official syrup and hot sauce of the Montgomery Biscuits.” I have no idea when I first started using (back in Carbondale, Illinois?), but it’s what I prefer for all my syrup needs. Imagine my delight that you can buy Alaga by by the case, only $30 for 12 24-ounce bottles (plus shipping). So, just in case you’re wondering what to gift me with, you know what to do; sometimes it’s hard to find this syrupy wonder in the North.
