Happy Birthday, Dad!

By willwhim

February 26, 2006

My father celebrates his 75th birthday on Monday, February 27. Three of my four brothers were able to come to a party to help him celebrate: from left to right: Ed, Dave, Dan, me; Dad (Harold) in the center. Steve attended virtually. Kudos to his nursing home, Tendercare of Westwood , for helping make this happen. (Also there: Dan’s wife Lynn, Dave’s wife Joanie, Ed’s wife Amy and son Davie, Bess, Mark and Jane.)
