Whitelisting in Google Mail

Google Mail (Gmail) has been putting mail from the LinkedIn contacts and groups into the spam folder. I had to research how to whitelist domains in Gmail, and this is what I learned: Press the ‘Create a Filter’ Enter the domain in the ‘From’ field Select the ‘Never send it to Spam’ option And, hey presto: it’s been whitelisted.
