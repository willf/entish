Qual question: What is the worst poem?

Daughter Jane has an assignment to “savor [the] awfulness” of a very bad poem, so we got to read some one unto another. Seamus Cooney has a Bad Poetry page, but there isn’t much that’s truly awful there, except perhaps, Kalamazoo (bottom of page) by JB Smiley: Kalamazoo On the outskirts are celery marshes Which only a few years ago Were as wet as a drugstore in Kansas And as worthless as marshes could grow, Well some genius bethought him to drain them And to add in a short year or two About eighty-five thousand dollars To the income of Kalamazoo.
