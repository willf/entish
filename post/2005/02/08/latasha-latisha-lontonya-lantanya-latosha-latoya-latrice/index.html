Latasha, Latisha, Lontonya, Lantanya, Latosha, LaToya, Latrice

By willwhim

February 8, 2005

Man o’ man is this cool: interactive chart of name popularity.. The chart here shows the popularity of names starting with LAT; it was interesting to see the decrease in people named ‘Jane,’ (our daughter’s name) over time–it really does seem like an ‘old lady’s name,’ as Jane often complains. No Britneys before the 1970s, or Khalils before the 1980s. (The chart only has data for the 1000 most popular names per decade). Via Metafilter.
