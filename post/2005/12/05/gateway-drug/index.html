Gateway drug?

By willwhim

December 6, 2005

It’s hard to believe I’ve never tried it before, but I finally did today, and I liked it. Maybe I’ll do it every Monday, then maybe I’ll try it on Tuesdays, and Wednesdays, and work up to the hard stuff on Sundays. It was fun, my wife turned me on to it. What I tried was pretty tame by her standards–she does the really hard stuff, stuff that takes over her brain sometimes for days at a time before it’s done and she can return to normal life.

Hah. Compared to her Atlantic Puzzler, the first New York Times Crossword that I’ve ever tried to complete on my own was nothing.
