How to put iPad in shuffle mode

By willwhim

October 6, 2010
  1. Launch iPod
  2. Select Music (on left rail)
  3. Select “Songs” (at bottom of screen)
  4. Drag entire list down (press some song and pull downwards). The “Shuffle” option will appear
  5. Press Shuffle

You can do this for any play list, including album songs. It’s surprising this is such a hidden option. Lots of my music are not “geniusable.”
