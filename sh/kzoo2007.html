9th Annual
Kalamazoo Annual Sacred Harp Singing
with Dinner on the Grounds
July 15, 2007
9:30 am to 3:00 pm EDT
Wesley Foundation on the campus of Western Michigan University
Free & Open to the Public. No Experience Required.
All are invited for a cappella singing of sacred songs, odes and anthems from The Sacred Harp, an American tunebook in print since 1844. Singing from the 1991 Denson Edition.
Driving Directions
Warning: Summer Roadwork Season!
Map to Location

Major roadwork at the intersection of I-94 and U.S. 131! All Oakland Drive exits on I-94 are closed, and Oakland Drive is closed to all traffic at I-94. Most of the I-94 to/from US 131 exits are closed.

  • US 131 (from Goshen): at exit #36A turn east onto Stadium Drive. Go 2 miles and turn left onto Howard Street. At the next light turn right onto Michigan Ave, then left onto Rankin Ave and follow signs to Wesley Foundation, just beyond the Fetzer Center, and on the right.
  • I-94 (from Chicago) at exit #72 turn north onto 9th Street, Oshtemo. Go about 1.5 miles to light at Stadium. After passing under US 131, proceed as above.
  • I-94 (from Detroit) at exit #82, follow BL-92 through to downtown Kalamazoo on King Highway, Kalamazoo Ave and Stadium Drive. Turn right on Howard. Turn right on Michigan Ave. and follow roundabout to Rankin Ave, follow signs to Wesley Foundation.
  • M-43 (W. Main): turn south onto Solon (one-way southbound) which becomes Howard St. Turn left at the light onto Michigan Ave then left onto Rankin as above.
