Key and Copyright information

  • Connexion: From the CD Lovely Vine: Selections from Jeremiah Ingalls' "The Christian Harmony".
  • Glimpse: From the CD "A Glimpse of Heavenly Day" Shape-note singing from Ingall's "The Christian Harmony"
  • Bicentennial: From Ingalls Bicentennial Singing, July 23rd 2005
  • Newfound Hills: Lesser known Ingalls tunes sung in harmony by Tom Malone.
  • Rain: From the CD "...A Gracious Rain..." recorded at the July 22, 2006 All-Day Singing in Newbury, VT.
